Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to start a photography business. I kind of do this project last semester, but I want to recreate a new one. On this website, I will introduce to my photography business, a gallery showcase to show different categories, and a contact page to let the user to submit their information.

Audience

For the audience, I will focus on college students who want to take professional pictures or graduation photos and young couples who want to get married, so they need a photographer to help them shoot wedding photos.

Dynamic elements

In this site, I will display a pop-up message when a user submits the contact form, toggle between different image categories in the gallery, and show different theme (light and dark) based on the time of day or switch by a interaction button.
